Omelette du fromage

Made with farm-fresh eggs and three cheeses, Le Restaurante's Omelette du fromage is a classic for any breakfast-lover. Enjoy the classic taste of scrambled eggs with Le Restaurante's twist, using fresh cracked pepper and salt and a sprinkling of fresh chives.

Krabby Patty

Nobody knows how to make a true Krabby Patty, but that won't stop Le Restaurante from trying. Made with a quarter pound of USDA Choice cut beef (and possibly a secret ingredient), our Krabby Patty is sure to make your mouth water like you're under the sea!
